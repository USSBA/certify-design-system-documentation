Access Requests (supervisor-access-requests.html)

  • On this page, you can accept or reject the applicant. The what happens to the sba user varies up on the selection made, so do both. Accepted users will turn green with the buttons replaced by the word "Accepted" and rejected users will turn red and have their buttons replaced by the word "Rejected"
  • The data points will only appear if that data exists. For MPP/WOSB/etc, since they won't indicate a duty station, the label for duty station will not appear.
  • After accepting/rejecting, refresh the page to see the null state.
